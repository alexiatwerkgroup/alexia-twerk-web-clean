The 12 best twerk creators on YouTube in 2026.

By Anti · firestarterApr 22, 202612 min read

Every Thursday we drop new videos across 7 curated playlists. Those videos don't appear from nowhere — they come from about 12 channels we watch religiously. This is that list, ranked by how often they land on our weekly selection as of April 2026.

#1 · WanGong Lin 林碗公 Taiwan

Runs the Taiwan Twerk Family Workshop. Teaches + choreographs at iDance Studio Taipei. His DaniLeigh "Tasty" choreo with Kerry Chiu is the gateway drug. If you see an Asian studio twerk video with 6 dancers in sync — that's his work.

#2 · Лада Гоцци Moscow

Moscow Dance Centre NRG's lead high-heels instructor. Her Pharaoh "Пломбир" video (167K views, 3,360 likes) is the most-played cut across our archive. Pure technique, zero gimmicks.

#3 · Nika Chill Los Angeles

Based LA, brings guest choreographers like Taylor Manning in. Chill Cheeks series is the best beginner curriculum on YouTube. 10 free lessons, zero filler.

#4 · Abasheva Yana / TWERKIT Studio Russia

The Rihanna "Needed Me" choreo (904K views) launched 1,000 imitators. Yana's beginner program is where half the Russian scene started.

#5 · Kato Dance Studio Germany

Ciri's choreo work. Nicki Minaj "Barbie World," Latto "Wheelie" — polished stage numbers with strong ensemble work. Best production values on the German scene.

#6 · Lena Indica Russia

Indica team runs workshops across the EU. Heels choreo specialist — Tinashe "Angels" is essential viewing. Dropping new content every 2-3 weeks.

#7 · Debii Abreu Dominican Republic

Latin flavor meets technique. Reyanna Maria "So Pretty" is her most replayed. Runs workshops across DR and Puerto Rico.

#8 · Yurgenis · YUR AULAR Venezuela

Reggaetón dance king. 22K likes on the Ghetto Kids "Baja" choreo alone. Brings latinx energy the rest of the list can't match.

#9 · Angela · iDance Studio Taipei

The other half of the iDance Taipei roster. Iggy Azalea "Sip It," Big Boss Vette "Pretty Girls Walk" — sharp, confident, cinematic camera work.

#10 · FOXYEN Taipei

Third iDance staple. Chlöe "Have Mercy" choreo is textbook. Never a wasted frame in her videos.

#11 · BEFOX Dance Studio Korea

K-pop stripdance crossover. FOX Choreography on Sam Smith "Unholy" is the best example. Pushes the boundary of what "twerk" means.

#12 · Emiliano Ferrari Villalobo Argentina

Argentinian reggaetón instructor. 2.8M views on "Me Gusta El Reggaetón." If you're in Buenos Aires, his Dance is Convey classes are the way in.

Why these 12 and not others?

Three filters: frequency (posts monthly or better), 4K or better camera work, and zero filler content. No "training day 127" vlog-style uploads — every video is a choreographed number with intent.

What to watch first.

If you've got 30 minutes: start with #1 WanGong Lin's "Tasty" (Taiwan workshop), jump to #2 Лада Гоцци's "Pharaoh Пломбир," close with #3 Nika Chill + Taylor Manning's class. You'll have seen three continents of the scene and found your lane.
