Weekly drop calendar 2026.

By Anti · firestarterApr 24, 20266 min read

Every Thursday, 00:00 ART (Argentina Time = UTC-3). Seven playlists. One hour window where the first 100 members get a token bonus. This is how it works.

What drops.

  1. Main archive (TTL) — 2-4 new videos added to the 275-video signature playlist.
  2. Korean / K-dance — 1-2 new cuts (iDance Taipei, BEFOX, Moscow-NRG when they feature Asian studio guests).
  3. Cosplay fancam — 3-5 new con-floor cuts from the previous weekend's conventions.
  4. Try-on haul — 1-2 new cuts, rotating creators.
  5. Latina model — 1-2 new cuts from Medellín/Bogotá/Caracas.
  6. Twerk Hub leaks — the curated greatest-hits channel — 1 cut/week.
  7. VIP-only drop — 1 exclusive cut that never appears in any public playlist. VIP Top members get access instantly, Premium 48h later.

The first-100 bonus.

The first 100 logged-in members to open a drop within the first 60 minutes earn +100 tokens. No skill, no gamble — just timing. That's why the home page shows a countdown to the next drop, so you don't miss it.

Preview window.

Medium tier members get a 12-hour preview: thumbnails + first 15 seconds of each new cut visible from Wednesday 12:00 ART through Thursday 00:00. You can't play full video yet, but you can save to your watch queue.

Premium tier members get the full video 48h before the public Thursday drop. VIP Top members get everything 72h early.

Why Thursday midnight ART?

Three reasons. (1) Argentina is the founding country — we're a Buenos Aires operation. (2) 00:00 ART hits mid-evening US east coast + 3am EU + noon Asia, so no major timezone is asleep. Either you catch the drop as it lands or you wake up to it. (3) Thursday positions the drop for Friday / weekend consumption — the peak of booty-dance content traffic globally.

What happens if a drop is delayed?

Hasn't happened since July 2022. If it did, active members would get a notification via email + Discord 60 min before deadline. The first-100 bonus window would shift accordingly.

How to never miss one.

  • Enable push notifications on the PWA (install Twerkhub to your home screen first — menu → "Install app").
  • Join the Discord lounge — the #drops channel pings 15 min before every Thursday drop.
  • Add the home-screen countdown — visible on every page of the site. Tracks to the second.
