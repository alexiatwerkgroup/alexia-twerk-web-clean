How to earn 10,000 tokens in 3 weeks.

By Anti · firestarter Apr 24, 2026 8 min read

Most Twerkhub members pay $9.99/mo for Medium, $29.99 for Premium, or $99.99 for VIP Top. But the token economy was designed so you can get VIP Top for free in about three weeks if you know the path. This is that path.

The tier thresholds, exactly.

Where tokens come from.

  • Page explore: +5 tokens per new page visited (max 10 pages/day = 50).
  • Watch a clip: +15 tokens per clip started (max 20 clips/day = 300).
  • Finish a clip: +30 tokens on top of the watch bonus (90% threshold).
  • Daily login: +50 flat, regardless of activity.
  • Streak bonus: Day 2 +60, Day 3 +75, Day 7 +200, Day 14 +400, Day 30 +1,000. Streak breaks if you miss a calendar day.
  • Referral: +500 per friend who registers + watches 5 clips.
  • Weekly drop engagement: first 100 users to watch a Thursday drop get +100.

The 21-day path, day by day.

Week 1 · Build the streak (target: 2,000 tokens)

Log in every day for 7 days. Watch 10 clips/day, finish 5 of them. Browse 10 pages each visit.

  • Daily login + explore + watch + finish: ~370 tokens/day baseline
  • Week 1 streak stack: 50+60+75+90+110+140+200 = 725
  • 7 × 370 + 725 = ~3,315 tokens

You're already Premium by Day 6. Keep going.

Week 2 · Compound (target: 6,000 tokens)

Don't break the streak. Invite 3 friends — each brings +500 once they watch 5 clips.

  • Week 2 streak: 240+280+320+360+400+440+400 = 2,440
  • Daily activity: 7 × 370 = 2,590
  • 3 referrals × 500 = 1,500
  • Weekly drop bonus (Thursday, if in first 100): +100
  • Total end of Week 2: 3,315 + 2,440 + 2,590 + 1,500 + 100 = ~9,945 tokens

Week 3 · The final push (target: 10,000+)

You need 55 more tokens. A single page-explore does it. You're VIP Top by Day 15.

Common mistakes that kill the streak.

  • Missing a day. The streak counter resets to 1 and you lose the Day 14 / Day 30 big unlocks.
  • Not finishing clips. The +30 completion bonus doubles your per-clip income.
  • Ignoring referrals. 500 each, no cap. Five friends = 2,500 free tokens.
  • Skipping Thursday drops. First 100 bonus = 100 tokens for watching anyway.

What VIP Top actually gets you.

Full 1,500+ video archive. Custom content priority (Alexia does your requests first). Direct Discord line — not a support channel, the actual creator DM. Monthly exclusive clips that never hit the main archive. Early drops 48h before Medium tier sees them.

The $99.99/mo price tag is real. Tokens are just another way in.
