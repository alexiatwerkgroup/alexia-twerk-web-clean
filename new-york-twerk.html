New York Twerk · NYC Scene

The studio circuit. Broadway choreographers crossing into commercial dance, Latin floor in Bushwick and the Bronx. NYC twerk, curated.

New York's twerk scene is studio-trained. Most of the city's working choreographers come out of Broadway, MTV-era commercial dance, or Alvin Ailey-style technique — and crossed into twerk and floor-work through reggaetón, hip-hop and Caribbean nights. Studios in Bushwick, the Bronx and Lower East Side run weekly classes. The Latin floor concentrates around Bushwick warehouse parties, summer rooftop sets, and the Spanish-speaking creator pipeline.

If you're searching 'twerk classes NYC', 'twerk near me New York', 'NYC twerk studios', or 'best twerk dancers in New York' — this is the index. Twerkhub adds verified NYC creators every week. The full national roster is at /creators-usa.html · the country hub at /usa-twerk.html.
