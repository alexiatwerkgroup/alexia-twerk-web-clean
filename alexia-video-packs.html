How pricing is framed

These packs are not presented as disposable content bundles. They are positioned as controlled access to a private archive, which means the comparison matters: historical custom pricing, current per-video value and the convenience of receiving a curated selection instead of negotiating one by one.

The goal is to make the decision easy without making the archive feel cheap. Starter is the entry. Extended is the strongest value jump. Legacy is the serious-buyer tier.
