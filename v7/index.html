§ 01
HOUSE CODE
The four pillars

A quiet house,
built for the long game.

Read the full charter →
i.

Curation

Every name on the roster has been signed in person. We screen for craft, not for clicks. Casting is closed nine months of the year — we choose when we open.

ii.

Verification

Every plate in our archive is verified at the source. Names, ages, releases, ID — all on file. The seven divisions ship without a single uncleared frame.

iii.

Representation

We negotiate hard, sign contracts in writing, and never split commission below market floor. Talent first, always. The house is built on the names we represent.

iv.

Custodianship

Eight years on. We still hold the original masters of the first cut we ever shot. Nothing leaves the vault without a clean release. The archive is for the house.
