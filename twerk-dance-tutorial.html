Tutorial intent (how-to searches)

People searching tutorials want clear structure. This page answers that intent and keeps them inside your site with internal links.

Core technique: the 4 layers

  • Posture: neutral spine, soft knees, stable upper body
  • Isolation: hips move independently from shoulders
  • Tempo: match movement to bpm (beats per minute)
  • Transitions: clean switch between slow control and fast bursts

Beginner vs advanced keywords

Beginner searches

Advanced searches
