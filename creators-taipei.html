Why Taipei?

Taiwan adopted the American studio-dance model in the early 2010s and then crossed it with K-pop methodology — 8-count phrasing, mirror formations, static wide camera. iDance in particular invested in production: color-graded 4K, multi-angle edits, consistent aesthetic across 200+ videos. No other city produces this volume of twerk content at this quality level.
