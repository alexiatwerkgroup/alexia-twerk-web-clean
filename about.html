/ The story · Est. 2018

Built for the uncut ones.

Twerkhub started in a Buenos Aires apartment in 2018. One creator, one platform builder, one question: what would a creator economy look like if the algorithm didn't decide what you saw? Seven years later we curate 1,500+ private 4K videos across seven playlists for a members community of 2,100+.

What we believe.

Creator-first economics. No ad revenue. No algorithm-driven feed. Every dollar from members goes to the people making the content. Tokens reward attention, not purchases.

Curation over volume. We don't index every video on the internet. Seven playlists, hand-picked, weekly drops. Quality > quantity — always.

Members not audience. Twerkhub is semi-private by design. You earn access via tokens or subscription. No casual visitors, no engagement farming.

Privacy as default. No public profiles. No social graph. No leaking who watches what. You come, you watch, you leave — we don't broadcast.

How it works.

Browse the 7 curated playlists. Earn tokens by exploring, watching, finishing clips, logging in. Hit thresholds: Basic (0-499) · Medium (500-1999) · Premium (2000-9999) · VIP Top (10000+). Or subscribe directly — $9.99/mo Medium, $29.99/mo Premium, $99.99/mo VIP Top.
