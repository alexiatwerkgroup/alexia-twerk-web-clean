Лада Гоцци

Lada Gozzi
Moscow · Russia MDC NRG Heels Instructor 167K · 3,360 likes

Bio

Лада Гоцци is the lead heels-choreography instructor at Moscow Dance Centre NRG — the Moscow studio that shaped the entire modern Russian heels-dance curriculum. Her Pharaoh "Пломбир" video (167K views, 3,360 likes, 5.00/5 rating) is the most-replayed cut across our main signature archive.

She teaches at MDC NRG's Moscow location (Planeta Leto mall, 3rd floor). Workshops are open-enrollment every month. The technique is formal: 8-count phrasing, mirror formation, fixed camera angle. No improvisation tolerated in class.

Technique signature

Three marks of a Лада piece: (1) 4-inch heels minimum — she doesn't teach flats; (2) hip pop lands on the 1-and, never on the downbeat — that's the Moscow school rhythm; (3) static feet, all motion in the hip isolation plus occasional shoulder roll.

What makes her the reference: precision. Every Лада video you watch, you can trace the phrase count-by-count. She's not freestyling — she's executing a rehearsed routine with no margin for error.

The reference cut

Pharaoh — "Пломбир". Russian pop/rap 2017. The song is everything the Moscow heels scene rides: slow tempo, dembow-ish pattern, space for dramatic pauses. Лада's choreo for it lives at position #130 of our archive but also sits at the #1 hot ranking of the main playlist.

Studio

Moscow Dance Centre NRG has 30+ groups across 20 styles. Лада runs the heels-choreo track. Contact the studio via their VK page (vk.com/mdcnrg) or WhatsApp (+7 915 109 98 89). Open year-round. Тел. in Moscow: 8 (985) 779 58 68.
