Kato Dance Studio

Bio

Kato Dance Studio (KDS) is the standout German twerk studio on YouTube. Production values are the highest on the European scene — multi-angle camera, crisp audio, color-graded footage. Their signature aesthetic: stage choreography that could open a music video tour.

Two resident choreographers do most of the uploads: Ciri (Nicki Minaj "Barbie World," polished stage pieces) and Mira (Latto "Wheelie," tighter club-floor pieces). Edits by Jasmin, who's been with KDS since 2021.

Technique signature

Three marks: (1) ensemble-first — solo pieces are rare, 4-6 dancers in formation is the default; (2) stage sensibility — camera framing + phrasing assumes a live audience, not a phone viewer; (3) production discipline — every upload goes through the full KDS pipeline (choreograph → rehearse → film → Jasmin edit). No raw content.

Classes cycle monthly. Not the cheapest drop-in in Europe but one of the most polished.

Why KDS matters

Most European twerk studios produce handheld Instagram content. KDS treats every upload as a TV deliverable. That standard pulled up the European scene's baseline over 2022-2025. What iDance Taipei is to Asia, Kato is to Europe.
