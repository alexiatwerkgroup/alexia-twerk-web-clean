Bio

Jutty leads Jutty's Twerk Team out of Poland and is one of the most-traveled choreographers in the Eastern European twerk circuit. Her catalogue alternates between team pieces (recorded in studio with her students) and guest cuts at international camps — most notably Obsession Twerk Camp Kyiv, Slay The Event Poland, and one-off collabs across the region.

Her teaching style is the bridge between Russian-trained discipline and the looser Western class format: she teaches phrases the same way Fraules-school instructors do (clean repeatable counts), but with the room-energy of a US class.

Technique signature

Three marks of a Jutty piece: (1) collab-friendly — almost half her cuts are guest spots with another instructor, which is rare; (2) two-camera shoots — wide for the team, close for her, edited together; (3) medium tempos — she avoids both ultra-slow floor work and rapid-fire freestyle, sitting comfortably in the middle where most pop-trap tracks live.
