Emiliano Ferrari Villalobo

Zárate · ArgentinaDance is ConveyReggaetón instructor2.8M views

Bio

Emiliano Ferrari Villalobo is the single most-viewed Argentine reggaetón choreographer on YouTube. His "Me Gusta El Reggaeton BONUS" upload from 2016 has 2.8M+ views, 9,391 likes, and is still the reference piece for Spanish-language reggaetón teaching in 2026.

He teaches at Dance is Convey in Zárate — a city about 90km north of Buenos Aires. The studio is not big or flashy. The teaching is. His Spanish-language breakdowns of reggaetón technique are the closest thing to a free university-level course the genre has in its own language.

Technique signature

Three marks: (1) ensemble pieces — his most-watched cuts feature 4-8 students in formation, not solos; (2) wide outdoor or studio framing — he never shoots tight; (3) pedagogy-friendly phrasing — every combination is teachable. His students can reproduce what they see. That's a deliberate choice — he's a teacher first, a choreographer second.

Reference cut

"Me Gusta El Reggaeton" BONUS track, January 2016. 2.8M views. Zárate - Buenos Aires - Argentina. The outdoor wide-shot version that became the "this is how reggaetón is taught" reference video for Spanish-speaking dance students globally.

Studio · Dance is Convey

Located in Zárate, Buenos Aires Province. Facebook + Instagram: @danceisconvey (transmiti-baila). Open enrollment. Student pipeline: Dance is Convey → BsAs Palermo studios → professional circuit.

For Spanish-speaking students wanting to learn reggaetón technique without leaving South America, his free YouTube library beats any paid course.
