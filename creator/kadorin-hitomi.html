Bio

Kadorin Hitomi is a Thailand-based cosplayer who works the full East Asian convention circuit — Seoul's regional cons, Taipei's FF series, Bangkok's PF events, occasional Japan trips. She brought the cosplay fancam format across a geography most creators never cover.

Her NIKKE (the video game) commander cosplay at PF40 is the most-replayed Kadorin cut. What separates her from other cosplayers: she commits to the choreography, not just the pose. Other cosplayers strike static poses for photo-ops. Kadorin executes a 30-60 second character-in-motion piece — that's fancam in the full sense.

Technique signature

Three marks: (1) character-driven choreo — the moves reference the character's in-universe gestures, not generic twerk; (2) long takes — 60-90 seconds of single-camera movement with no cuts; (3) crowd-tolerant — she performs with convention-floor ambient chaos around her without breaking character.

Reference cuts

NIKKE · PF40. Commander cosplay, full character-choreo run. Thailand convention circuit. The cut is on our at position #004.
