Street Dance Greece

9.9M+ views Greece Thessaloniki Battle organizer 7 cuts indexed

Bio

Street Dance Greece is the Thessaloniki-based dance organization behind Twerk Battle Vol.2 — the most-replayed Greek twerk event on YouTube. The Vol.2 edition produced one of the highest-view solo cuts in the entire archive: Yota PL's judge solo at 7.6M views. ANEL LI's parallel judge solo did 1.6M.

Beyond the battles, the organization runs the Greek twerk and street-style scene out of Thessaloniki, with regular invitations to international twerk teams (Anel Li's Empire crew, traveling judges from Russia and Italy).

Technique signature

Three marks of a Street Dance Greece cut: (1) battle format — judge solos are short, dense, performance pieces (60-90 seconds of full-energy choreo); (2) large stage shoots — they record on event stages with concert-grade lighting, which separates them visually from studio cuts; (3) international guest panel — judges rotate, so the catalog is also a record of who was touring Greece each year.
