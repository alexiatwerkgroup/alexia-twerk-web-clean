FOXYEN

Taipei · TaiwaniDance StudioChoreographer#3 Taipei

Bio

Third staple of the iDance Studio Taipei roster. FOXYEN operates slightly behind WanGong Lin and Angela in the studio's attention hierarchy, but her technique rewards closer study. The Chlöe "Have Mercy" choreo is the textbook K-dance twerk reference: sharp counts, controlled isolations, confident floor work.

Technique signature

What separates FOXYEN: she's a slower dancer by design. Her phrasing sits on the beat, not ahead of it. That slight delay reads as confidence. The hip pop lands a millisecond after you expected — which makes it hit harder.

She also borrows from jazz-funk more than the other iDance instructors. Her shoulder work + arm phrasing are richer, more dramatic. If you want to study heels choreo with story arc, FOXYEN is the path.

Reference cuts

Chlöe — "Have Mercy". The textbook. Solo performance, studio master shot, full phrase. Lives at position #019 of our main archive.
