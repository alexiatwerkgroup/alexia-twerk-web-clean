Dasha Kolesnikova

Ufa · RussiaSolo dancerOutdoor fancamRoster · 2024

Bio

Dasha Kolesnikova is a Russian dancer based in Ufa, the capital of Bashkortostan. She doesn't have a studio. She doesn't have a manager. What she has is a rooftop with a good view, a partner who knows how to operate a camera, and a metronome of weekly uploads since 2022. The combination produced one of the most-replayed cuts in our archive.

Her Nicki Minaj "Yikes" choreography on a Ufa rooftop sits at 136K views and is the reference piece for what we call regional Russian fancam — work produced outside the Moscow / St Petersburg studio circuit, often outdoors, on phones or single-camera rigs, by dancers who built audiences without institutional support.

Technique signature

Three marks of a Dasha Kolesnikova piece. (1) Outdoor framing. Most uploads use a Ufa rooftop, balcony, or industrial yard as set. Her camera operator (her partner) frames wide enough to include the city behind her — the geography is part of the brand. (2) Tight execution despite no studio. Her isolations are MDC-school clean. She trained at local Ufa studios in 2019-2021 and the technique base is solid. (3) Single-take honesty. No multi-angle edits. One camera, one take. Reupload-resistant by design.

Reference repertoire

Strongest cuts cluster around late-2010s American hip-hop — Nicki Minaj, Megan Thee Stallion, Cardi B — plus periodic Russian-language work (Скриптонит, Хаски). The Nicki Minaj catalog is where she does her best work; the Russian-language pieces have higher local reach but lower global viewership.

Why she's on the roster

The Russian scene's center of gravity is Moscow + St Petersburg. Regional dancers — Ufa, Volzhsky, Omsk, Yekaterinburg — produce real work but rarely break into international audiences because they're not embedded in the studio brands that distribute. Dasha is the cleanest counter-example. Her presence on the roster signals that we cover the regional half of the Russian scene, not just the Moscow studios.

Where to find her

YouTube channel weekly. VK + Telegram for behind-the-scenes. Doesn't take commercial work. Doesn't run paid sessions. Self-directed, partner-operated, regional by choice.
