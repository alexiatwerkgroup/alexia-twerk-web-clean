Sav

United States Maine + LA Twerk choreographer Class & freestyle 45+ cuts

Bio

Sav — known online as TheQueenBSav or Sav the Booty Queen — runs one of the most consistent twerk class catalogs out of New England. Her home base is Maine; she travels regularly to Los Angeles to teach drop-in classes and shoot collab cuts with the LA twerk circuit (Nastya Nass, Destiny Vaughan, and the rest of that scene).

She doesn't do glossy editorial. Her brand is class energy — phones up, laughs in the room, the camera catches whoever happened to be in front. That low-friction format is also why her catalogue is so dense: 45+ cuts in the Twerkhub archive, half class choreographies, half freestyles to whatever track is breaking.

Technique signature

Three marks of a Sav piece: (1) punchy 8-counts — she favours short repeatable phrases over long stories, which is why her stuff teaches well; (2) vocal cues on tape — she calls counts and adjustments mid-take, and the takes don't get edited out; (3) hard fronting — she holds the camera, no backing dancers stealing focus, no studio tricks. The result is something between a class clip and a music-video freestyle.
