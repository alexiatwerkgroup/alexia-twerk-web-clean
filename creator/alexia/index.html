About Alexia

Alexia is the creative force and founder behind Alexia Twerk Group and the Twerkhub platform. As both an artist and curator, Alexia has built a unique space for documenting, celebrating, and advancing twerk culture globally. Her work bridges performance, digital media, and community engagement.

The platform under her direction serves as a research-grade archive of contemporary twerk and movement culture, featuring verified creator profiles, original content, and community-driven features.

Creative Direction

Alexia's approach to content curation and platform development is grounded in authenticity and cultural documentation. Key areas of focus:

Platform Vision

Twerkhub was built to solve a specific problem: there was no centralized, verified, high-quality archive of the creators and artists driving twerk culture. The platform combines artist profiles, verified links, performance documentation, and community features — all without compromising on authenticity or artist integrity.

Content Ethics

Alexia's curation philosophy emphasizes verified creators, official links only, and clear separation between the public figure and the marketed brand. No leaks, no impersonation, no shortcuts. Every profile on Twerkhub is research-grade: timelines, verifiable links, and contextualized work.

Community Building

Beyond the archive, Alexia has developed Twerkhub into a community platform where audiences can discover, follow, and support their favorite creators through verified channels. The membership tiers support direct artist compensation.
