Russian Twerk Roster

If twerk has a non-American capital, it's Russia. The country's choreographers built the genre's strongest studio infrastructure outside the US — large rooms, technical syllabuses, video documentation, international reach. The roster below covers Moscow, Novosibirsk and the Volga region.

The choreographers who put Russian twerk on the global map.
