How we pick names

Three filters: cultural reach (the creator the audience already knows by handle, not by full name), publication trail (a verifiable career outside of leak sites and fan re-uploads), and active operation (the creator is currently shipping work somewhere — TikTok, OF, Patreon, IG, YouTube — under the name we're using). If a candidate fails any of the three, they don't ship a page.
