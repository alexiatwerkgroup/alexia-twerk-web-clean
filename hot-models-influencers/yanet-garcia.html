Bio

Yanet García is the most globally followed weather presenter in modern television. Born November 15, 1990 in Monterrey, Nuevo León, she got her break on the local Telediario Monterrey weather desk around 2010 — a slot most networks fill with rotating talent and forget about. She didn't get forgotten. Within three years the desk segments started getting clipped and re-uploaded across YouTube and the early Latin American Twitter ecosystem, and by 2014 the international press had picked her up under the nickname "La Chica del Clima".

What followed is the standard Latin-influencer arc, but moving faster than most. Co-anchor invitations on Hoy and Despierta América. A move first to Mexico City and later between Los Angeles and Miami. The launch of her fitness brand YG Fitness. Endorsement deals across athletic apparel, swim, and supplements. By 2019 she had crossed 15 million Instagram followers — a number that, even adjusting for follower-count inflation in the era, places her in the top tier of Latin women on the platform.

Why she's on this roster

The Hot Models & Influencers section exists for a specific reason: there's a generation of Latin creators who came up before OnlyFans existed, built their careers in mainstream media, and then made the conscious choice to move part of their work into adult content. Yanet García is the cleanest example of that path. The 2020 OnlyFans launch wasn't a pivot — it was an expansion. She kept the fitness brand. She kept the IG. She kept the public co-host appearances. She added a separate channel for her adult work and ran them in parallel.

That choice matters because it set the template. Half of the creators who launched OF in the 2020-2022 wave cite her as the unlock — the proof that the move didn't have to torch a mainstream career.

Body of work

Three tiers, depending on platform. Instagram (free) is fitness-focused: gym videos, swimwear shoots in Tulum / Cabo / Miami Beach, brand activations. TikTok (free) trends-aware short-form — dance challenges, day-in-the-life, casual outfit-of-the-day. OnlyFans (paid) is the adult layer with explicit premium content gated behind subscription.

Style throughout is fitness-first, glamour-second. The shoot direction emphasizes natural light, Latin coastal environments, and athletic poses over studio glam. That visual signature is part of why the brand has aged well — most of her 2014 content still looks contemporary because it never leaned on a era-specific aesthetic.
