The short version

Andrea Espada is the rare profile on this roster that started in YouTube-family-channel territory before crossing into glamour modeling. Bogotá-born, Miami-based, co-creator of The Royalty Family YouTube channel — one of the most-subscribed Latin-creator channels on the platform — and a 10M-plus Instagram following on her solo handle. The career arc is the inverse of most of the names on this roster: prank-and-vlog YouTuber first, glamour-creator second.

Why she's on this roster

Three reasons. First, she's the cleanest example of YouTube-as-audience-source on this roster — a useful counter-reference to the IG-first names like Demi Rose and Karely Ruiz. Second, her Spanish-language audience overlaps with our Latin-American footprint, just like Karely and Aida do, but the audience composition is different (family-channel viewers are a different demographic than OF subscribers). Third, the cross-platform breadth — YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, modeling work — makes her a useful case study for anyone reading our blog content on the multi-platform creator economy.

How she compares to Twerkhub talent
