Street Project

Russia Volzhsky School 6 cuts

Bio

Street Project — full name Школа Танцев "Street Project" — is a dance school based in Volzhsky, Russia. They run express twerk courses (4-6 weeks) plus regular team choreographies.

Two recurring track choices in their catalog: TWERK / Shake Dat and 99 Percent / Yike In It with Monika as featured dancer. Group pieces are their signature — formations of 8-15 students.

Technique signature

Three marks: (1) express course format as content; (2) large group formations; (3) Russian-language pop + American trap mix.
