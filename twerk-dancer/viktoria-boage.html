Viktoria Boage

Velvet Young 36 cuts Choreographer Russia

Bio

Viktoria Boage is the most-prolific solo choreographer in the Velvet Young Dance Centre roster — 36 indexed cuts spanning AAP Ferg, Sueco, Iggy Azalea, Megan Thee Stallion, Karol G, J Balvin, Sean Paul, NK and the female-rap canon.

Her brand is Dance Intensive series (running ~3 years) plus solo Velvet Young choreographies. Strong technical floor work, fast counts, group execution.

Technique signature

Three marks of a Boage piece: (1) Dance Intensive serial numbering; (2) aggressive tempo; (3) track diversity — Latin, trap, female rap, Russian-language pop all in the same catalog.
