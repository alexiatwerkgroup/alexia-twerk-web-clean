Velvet Young Dance Centre

Velvet Young 54 cuts indexed Studio Twerk

Bio

Velvet Young Dance Centre is one of the largest twerk studio rosters indexed on Twerkhub — 54 cuts, multiple choreographers (Diana Petrosyan, Viktoria Boage, Melinda Efteni and others), and a recurring Dance Intensive series that traces back through the studio's history.

Catalog is dominated by trap/hip-hop tracks (Megan Thee Stallion, Cardi B, Iggy Azalea, Saweetie, Mulatto, Latto, Future, Young Thug) plus a strong reggaetón secondary line. Reference reference cut: "Shake Your Bam Bam" by RDX (61K views).

Technique signature

Three marks of a Velvet Young cut: (1) Dance Intensive numbering system (vol 1, vol 19, vol 22) — a serial format; (2) multi-choreographer rotation — Diana, Viktoria, Melinda + guests; (3) Russian polish + American track curation.
