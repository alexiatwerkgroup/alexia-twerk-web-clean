Latin American Twerk Roster

The Latin American roster is structured around reggaetón floor work and Caribbean perreo. The instructors below split time between Buenos Aires, Bogotá, Medellín, Santo Domingo, and a recurring Mexico City circuit. The technique signature of the region is slower-than-Russian, lower-to-the-floor, and tightly paired with the reggaetón kick pattern.
