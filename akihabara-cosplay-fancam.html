Akihabara Cosplay · Otaku District

The neighborhood that exports otaku culture. Maid cafés, idol scene, manga shops, and the densest cosplay floor on Earth.

Akihabara is the source code of global otaku culture. The neighborhood compresses anime, manga, gaming, idol culture, maid cafés and cosplay into a few square kilometers in central Tokyo. Cosplay-on-the-street is part of the daily walk here — and the fancam pipeline shooting it is unmatched in density. The idol scene (AKB48, plus a long tail of underground groups) feeds the cosplay calendar with year-round events.
